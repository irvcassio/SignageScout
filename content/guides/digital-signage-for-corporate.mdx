Corporate offices are the second-largest vertical market for digital signage after retail. From lobby welcome screens to meeting room signs, digital displays improve communication, reinforce brand culture, and streamline visitor experiences.

## Common Use Cases

### Lobby Directories and Visitor Welcome

Reception area screens greet visitors by name (integrated with visitor management systems), display company branding, show building directories, and provide wayfinding. First impressions matter — a polished digital lobby display signals a modern, professional organization.

### Meeting Room Signs

Small displays (10-15 inches) outside conference rooms show current booking, upcoming meetings, room availability, and allow on-the-spot reservations. This eliminates the "is this room free?" problem.

### Employee Communications

Break room, kitchen, and corridor screens display company news, HR announcements, benefits enrollment reminders, recognition programs, and social feeds. [Poppulo](/vendors/poppulo) (formerly Four Winds Interactive) is a market leader in corporate communications signage.

### KPI Dashboards

Engineering floors, sales bullpens, and operations centers use large displays showing real-time KPIs from tools like Power BI, Tableau, Google Data Studio, or custom dashboards. [ScreenCloud](/vendors/screencloud) integrates with 80+ data sources for live dashboard displays.

### Cafeteria and Amenity Information

Corporate dining facilities use digital menu boards for daily specials, nutritional information, and wait times. Fitness center screens show class schedules and occupancy.

## Why Corporate Offices Invest in Digital Signage

- **63% of employees** say digital signage catches their attention more than email
- **Meeting room signs** reduce double-bookings by 30-40%
- **Visitor management integration** streamlines check-in and improves security
- **Real-time dashboards** keep teams aligned without interrupting workflow

## Integration Points

Corporate signage is most powerful when connected to existing systems:

**Calendar systems** — Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace provide meeting room data for room signs and event displays.

**Visitor management** — Systems like Envoy, Proxyclick, or Teem push visitor welcome messages to lobby screens.

**Business intelligence** — Power BI, Tableau, and Looker dashboards render on digital displays for real-time KPIs.

**HR and intranet** — SharePoint, Workplace from Meta, and Slack channels feed content to employee communication screens.

**Building systems** — Occupancy sensors, HVAC status, and elevator displays for smart building integration.

## Deployment Strategy

### Phase 1: High-Impact Locations (2-4 weeks)
- Lobby welcome screen + building directory
- 5-10 meeting room signs for most-used conference rooms
- Break room communication screen

### Phase 2: Department Rollout (4-8 weeks)
- KPI dashboards for key departments
- Additional meeting room signs
- Cafeteria menu boards

### Phase 3: Campus-Wide (8-12 weeks)
- Elevator lobby screens on every floor
- Parking garage wayfinding
- Fitness center and amenity displays
